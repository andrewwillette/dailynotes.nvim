*dailynotes.nvim* Configure creation and editing of daily notes.

dailynotes.init                                 *dailynotes.init(filetype)*

Configure the filetype to be used for dailynotes.

dailynotes.addDailyNoteShortcut                  *dailynotes.addDailyNoteShortcut(keymap, directory)*

Configure a keymap to trigger dailynotes in the directory.
